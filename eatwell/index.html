Welcome

App-pe-diet is a healthy food startup that provides customised nutritious meals to its customers on a subscription based model. We cater to the needs of a niche customer base who are unable to cook but requires customized nutritious diets for reasons like losing weight, diabetics, strength training or just maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

The idea of App-pe-diet was born from the personal experiences of our team members. When we asked ourselves what is the biggest issue that we face every day, the answer was healthy food. There are not many healthy food options available out there which can be consumed all three times of the day everyday of the week, available at a reasonable price and easily accessible. Even if you can source healthy food, it is highly unlikely that it will be according to your taste preferences, your dietary requirements and in the right quantity. This is the problem we have faced, many young professionals out there are facing and this is the problem we are trying to solve through App-pe-diet.
